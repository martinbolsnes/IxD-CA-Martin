Care Global

At Care Global you can donate to many different charities at the same time. We have made a site that lets you select the charities you want to donate to and the amount you want to donate. You can choose to donate a one-time amount, or you can add charities to your donations list and create a monthly payment. The donations list lets you set a monthly amount that you can assign to the different charities you have on your list.

Donating have never been easier!
